What we monitor

Six signals. One score.

Layoffs & reductions

WARN filings and verified announcements. Reach displaced talent days before it hits LinkedIn.

Funding rounds

Seed through IPO. New capital precedes headcount growth by 60–90 days.

Hiring surges

Velocity spikes across job boards. When a company opens 20+ roles in a month, something shifted.

Hiring freezes

Detected via posting drops, earnings calls, and public announcements.

Leadership changes

C-suite departures often precede reorgs, rebuilds, and fresh sourcing budgets.

M&A & acquisitions

Mergers create volatility in both directions — new headcount needs and redundancy exits.

How it works

An agent that never stops watching

01

Continuous ingestion

47+ public sources scanned every 15 minutes — WARN filings, funding DBs, job boards, news, GitHub.

02

Signal classification

Each event is typed and weighted. A WARN filing in engineering reads differently than one in marketing.

03

Company scoring

Every company gets a 0–100 talent availability score, updated continuously from all recent signals.

04

Ranked sourcing queue

Your dashboard surfaces the highest-signal companies first. Filter by industry, size, or signal type.
